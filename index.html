Getting Access
How to get in and start your first session

Currently open to the DG team. PMM and Engagement onboarding coming when those brains go live.

1
Get Claude Pro

Projects require Claude Pro ($20/mo). Ping Clay to confirm if the team covers it.

2
Get Added

DG Brain: ping Clay Parker. Marketing HQ: ping Shawn.

3
Open from the Project

Log in, click Projects in left sidebar, open EZO DG Brain, start there.

⚠️ Never from the homepage — starts blank.
4
Use the Opener

Paste the prompt below at the start of every session to reload context.

⚠️

Always open sessions from inside the Project — not from the Claude homepage. The homepage creates a blank session with no memory. The brain won't know who you are or what we do.

Paste this at the start of every session
"You are the EZO DG Brain. Start by reviewing the project files to refresh your context, then confirm what you know about current campaigns, our brand voice, and any open priorities. What's top of mind?"
